6. *Josei* (, lit. "young woman") - Violet Evergarden, Carole & Tuesday (more mature themes, targeted towards a female audience)
7. *Isekai* (, lit. "different world") - Maoyū Maō Yūsha (transported to another world)
8. *Dark Fantasy* (, dāku fantajī) - Sirius the Jaeger, Mononoke
9. *Slice-of-Life* (, seikatsu no katachi) - Polar Bear Cafe, Yumeiro Pâtissière
10. *Historical* (, rekishi) - some elements in Moribito, The Mystic Archives of Dantalian
